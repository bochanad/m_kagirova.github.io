Hello, I'm Maria

I paint the places I travel to, on location.

Not the landmarks — the café table, the cup, the dessert I was too curious not to order. Painting on the spot is how I remember a place properly: the light, the queue, the word a stranger taught me.

Every page here is yours to take home as a print, or as the one and only original.

— Maria
