    hello, I'm Kagi

    An artist who travels with a tiny box of paint.

    I never set out to draw landmarks. I draw the table I'm sitting at — the café, the cup, the dessert I'm too curious not to order. Painting on location is how I remember a place properly: the colour of the light, how long the queue was, the word a stranger taught me.

    I've filled my sketchbook across Asia, the Middle East, Europe and the Americas. This is where those pages live now — yours to take home as a print, or as the one and only original.
